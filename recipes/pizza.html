PIZZA from Italy

What pizza really is

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese, typically with added meat or vegetables. It’s enjoyed worldwide for its delicious taste and variety of flavors.

What ingredients are needed?

  • Pizza Dough
    This is made from all-purpose flour or bread flour, yeast, and water12
  • Pizza Sauce
    This is usually a tomato-based sauce that is spread on the pizza dough
  • Cheese
    Mozzarella is the most common cheese used on a pizza
  • Toppings
    These can vary widely, but common ones include vegetables like onions, tomatoes, and capsicums4, and meats. Some pizzas also have additional ingredients like olives, mushrooms, and various types of cheese

How is it made?

  1. Prepare the Dough
    Mix flour, yeast, and water to form a soft, fairly wet dough. Knead it for about 5 minutes until it’s smooth, then cover it with a tea towel and set it aside
  2. Prepare the Sauce
    Mix passata (a type of tomato puree), basil, and crushed garlic together, then season it to taste
  3. Shape the Pizza and Add Toppings
    Roll out the dough into a large round shape, about 25cm across. Spread the sauce over the base with the back of a spoon, then scatter cheese and your chosen toppings
  4. Bake the Pizza
    Preheat your oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put the pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of a preheated sheet or tray in the oven. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the crust is crisp
